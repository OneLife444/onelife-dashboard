Strategic Notes

🏗️ Construction Industry — Pitch as Pipeline, Not Charity
IWC Excavation, Knappett Industries, and VICA's 450+ member companies need reliable workers. One Life produces employment-ready men in recovery. Frame this conversation as a workforce partnership — it's more sustainable than any grant cycle and opens doors to multiple companies simultaneously.
🤝 Indigenous Partnerships Unlock Hidden Funding
One Life cannot apply for FNHA Harm Reduction Grants ($50K) directly. But with Tillicum Lelum as a co-applicant, that funding becomes accessible. Building the Tillicum Lelum and Snuneymuxw relationships isn't just about referrals — it's the key to an entire additional funding stream.
⛪ One Parish Relationship = Three Funders
Connecting with Holy Trinity Catholic Parish unlocks three organizations simultaneously: the Catholic Foundation of Vancouver Island, Knights of Columbus Council 9990 (same building), and the Society of St. Vincent de Paul Nanaimo. One relationship introduction, three potential funding conversations.
📋 Service Clubs: One Presentation, Four Asks
Rotary (main club), Rotary Daybreak, Kiwanis Sunrisers, and Kinsmen can each receive the same presentation with minor tweaks. Brett should block two weeks to do all four club visits in sequence. Combined potential: $5,000–$40,000 with relatively low preparation effort.

What's Blocking Applications

📊
Program Outcomes Data
Completion rates, employment, sobriety, housing stability. Required for every grant narrative. Highest priority item blocking all applications.
💰
Financial Statements (2–3 years)
Required for Nanaimo Foundation, First West Foundation, and most formal funders. Also determines Lululemon grant cap eligibility.
🔢
CRA Charity Number (9-digit BN)
Required for all federal and most provincial applications. Currently only CanadaHelps ID (#122320) on file.
👥
Board of Directors List
Names and roles required on most formal grant applications. Diverse community representation strengthens applications significantly.
🏠
489 Seventh Street Explanation
BC registry shows as "Unregistered." Funders will ask. Needs a clear written explanation before submitting any provincial applications.
👤
Resident Age Breakdown
Determines TELUS eligibility (youth under 29) and strengthens demographic need statements in all grant narratives.
